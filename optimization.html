Optimization Insights
How It Works
The clustering algorithm groups faculty by shared subject expertise using a Jaccard similarity measure. Staff who share many common teaching areas are placed in the same cluster. Each cluster then identifies the best-fit subjects based on the group's collective expertise levels (High/Medium/Low).
Benefits
Reduced teaching load variance across faculty. Better matching of expertise to curriculum requirements. Identified gaps where additional hiring or training may be needed. Cross-branch course sharing opportunities highlighted.
